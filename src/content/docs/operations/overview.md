vBox Operations helps organizations improve reliability, performance, and governance across their Azure environment. It provides the Observability Score KPI, operational best-practice recommendations, and AzGovViz integration for policy and RBAC insights.

## Key Capabilities

- **Observability Score** — Track your monitoring maturity based on vBox best practices
- **Operations Recommendations** — Actionable recommendations for improving operational excellence
- **AzGovViz Integration** — Policy, RBAC, and management group insights via Azure Governance Visualizer
- **BI Dashboards** — Operational health reporting through embedded Metabase dashboards
