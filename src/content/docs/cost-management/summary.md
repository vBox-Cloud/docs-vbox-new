The Cost Summary page provides a high-level overview of your Azure spending through embedded BI dashboards powered by Metabase.

## Dashboard Charts

The Cost Summary dashboard includes the following visualizations:

- **Projected Annual Cost $** — Forecasted annual Azure spend
- **Annual Potential Saving %** — Percentage of potential savings identified
- **Annual Potential Saving $** — Dollar amount of potential savings
- **Cost Recommendations Strategies** — Breakdown of optimization strategies
- **Cost By Service Name** — Cost distribution across Azure services
- **Overall Tag Coverage** — Resource tagging compliance
- **Budget by Subscription** — Budget tracking per subscription

:::note
The Cost Summary page requires the [BI Analytics](/analytics/bi-analytics/) feature to be enabled.
:::
